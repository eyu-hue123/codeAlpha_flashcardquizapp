Title: Journey of a Design Intern

This storyboard illustrates the journey of a student who joins the CodeAlpha Graphic Designing Internship.

1. In the first scene, the student finds the internship announcement online and gets excited.
2. In the second scene, they register and receive detailed task instructions.
3. The third scene shows them learning Canva and Figma to begin their first task.
4. In the fourth scene, they create a logo and a storyboard to complete the internship.
5. The fifth scene shows them uploading the project and sharing it on LinkedIn.
6. Finally, the student receives the certificate and celebrates their achievement.

This storyboard captures the typical journey of a motivated intern learning design skills and growing professionally.
